Greenbean Casserole

Description

This is a classic greenbean casserole recipe by Campbell's.

Ingredients

  • 1 can of Campbell's Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup or Cream of Chicken
  • 4 cups of cut green beans
  • 1 1/3 cups of French's French Fried Onions

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Mix soup, green beans, and 2/3 cup onions in a casserole dish.
  3. Bake for 25 minutes.
  4. Stir, top with remaining onions, and bake 5 more minutes.

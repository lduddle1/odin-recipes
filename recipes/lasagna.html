Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for a traditional lasagna made by Holly at Spend With Pennies.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 36 ounces pasta sauce
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • two cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Cook noodles until al dente, drain, rinse, and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, brown sausage. Add pasta sauce, tomato paste, seasoning, 1/2 tsp salt, and simmer 5 mins.
  3. In a bowl, mix ricotta, egg, parsley, 1 1/2 cups mozzarella, 1/4 cup Parmesan, and 1/4 tsp salt.
  4. In a 9×13 dish, layer: 1 cup meat sauce, 3 noodles, 1/3 cheese mixture, 1 cup meat sauce. Repeat twice. Top with 3 noodles and remaining sauce.
  5. Cover with foil and bake 45 mins. Remove foil, top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan. Bake 15 mins more.
  6. Let rest 15 mins before serving.

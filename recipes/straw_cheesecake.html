Strawberry Cheesecake Recipe

Description

This is a fun strawberry cheesecake recipe by Nagi at Recipetineats.

Ingredients

Base

  • 200g plain biscuits
  • 120g unsalted butter, melted

Filling

  • 500g cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 eggs

Topping

  • 500g strawberries (half chopped, half halved)
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tsp lemon juice (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C / 320°F. Line a springform pan with baking paper.
  2. Crush biscuits into fine crumbs. Mix with melted butter. Press into the base and slightly up the sides of the pan. Chill while making the filling.
  3. Beat cream cheese until smooth. Mix in sour cream, sugar, and vanilla. Add eggs one at a time, mixing just until combined.
  4. Pour into crust.
  5. Bake for 55 minutes. The center should jiggle slightly. Cool in the oven with the door slightly open, then refrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  6. In a saucepan, cook chopped strawberries, sugar, and lemon juice over medium heat for 10 mins.
  7. Mix cornstarch with water, add to the pan, stir until thickened. Stir in halved strawberries and cook 1 more minute. Cool completely.
  8. Spoon cooled topping over cheesecake. Chill at least 2 hours. Slice and serve.
